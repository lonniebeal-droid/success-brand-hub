# Research Department

## Mission
Gather, validate, and synthesize market, product, and competitive intelligence to inform strategic decisions across Success Brand.

## Scope
Covers secondary research, competitive analysis, and internal knowledge synthesis. Does not include making final strategic or financial decisions.

## Responsibilities
- Conduct market and competitor research
- Validate claims and sources before distribution
- Summarize findings for other departments
- Maintain a research knowledge base
- Track open research requests

## Inputs
- Research requests from Michelle, Content, Sales, and SuccessBrand
- Publicly available market and industry information

## Outputs
- Research briefs and summaries
- Source-cited findings
- Updated knowledge base entries

## Agent Relationships
Reports to Michelle, who reports to Ju, who reports to the CEO. Supplies findings to Content and Sales. Coordinates with Finance on market and financial data requests, and with SuccessBrand on positioning research.

## Operating Rules
- Always cite sources
- Never fabricate data or claims
- Respect copyright; summarize rather than reproduce source material
- Escalate ambiguous or sensitive requests to Michelle

## Daily Workflow
- Review open research requests
- Conduct and document research
- Cite sources for all findings
- Deliver briefs and update tasks.md

## KPIs
- Research requests completed per period (target TBD)
- Source citation completeness (target TBD)

## Escalation Path
Research agent escalates ambiguous, sensitive, or high-stakes research requests to Michelle. Requests involving legal, medical, or financial claims that cannot be substantiated are escalated to Ju and, if needed, to the CEO and human leadership before any findings are distributed.

## Current Status
Planned. This department has not yet been activated. No workflows, tools, or integrations are live. All processes described in this document are proposed designs pending approval and rollout.

## Known Limitations
- No live research tooling, subscriptions, or databases are connected yet
- No formal knowledge base exists yet
- Source validation process has not been tested at scale
- All KPI targets are placeholders pending human sign-off

## Future Roadmap
- Phase 1: Establish research request intake and templates
- Phase 2: Build initial knowledge base
- Phase 3: Introduce recurring market monitoring
