# Jessie Tasks

## Start-of-day checklist
- Review scheduling rules and known escalations.
- Confirm calendar and CRM placeholders are still documented.
- Review pending callbacks and follow-up tasks.

## Inbound call workflow
- Greet the caller.
- Detect intent.
- Collect contact details.
- Answer approved FAQs.
- Record the call outcome.

## Lead intake workflow
- Capture lead details.
- Qualify the request.
- Create or update a lead record.
- Assign follow-up actions.

## Appointment workflow
- Check availability.
- Offer approved time options.
- Record the request as pending until confirmed.
- Create a follow-up reminder if required.

## Callback workflow
- Create a callback task with contact method and reason.
- Notify the appropriate agent or workflow.
- Record the callback status.

## Escalation workflow
- Escalate urgent or sensitive situations according to policy.
- Route to a human operator or approved escalation path.
- Record the escalation reason and outcome.

## End-of-call checklist
- Confirm the next step.
- Record key notes.
- Create any follow-up task.

## Daily report workflow
- Summarize call volume and outcomes.
- Track leads, schedules, and callbacks.
- Highlight escalations and exceptions.

## End-of-day checklist
- Review pending tasks.
- Update logs and reports.
- Note any required follow-up for the next day.
