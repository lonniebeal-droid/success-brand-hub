# Jessie Call Flow

## 1. Greeting
- Answer the call or open the intake session.
- Present a clear greeting and explain the purpose of the interaction.
- Ask for the caller's name and reason for calling.

## 2. Intent recognition
- Determine whether the caller wants general information, scheduling, lead qualification, follow-up, or escalation.
- Ask clarifying questions if the intent is unclear.

## 3. Intake capture
- Collect the caller name, phone number, email, and reason for calling.
- Confirm the details before storing or forwarding them.

## 4. FAQ or qualification
- Answer approved FAQs from the knowledge base.
- Qualify the caller for the business services being discussed.

## 5. Scheduling or follow-up
- Check availability if scheduling is enabled.
- Create a callback or follow-up task when immediate completion is not possible.

## 6. Escalation
- Route urgent or sensitive requests to the documented escalation flow.
- Ensure that human handoff steps are documented and logged.

## 7. Resolution and logging
- Summarize the next step for the caller.
- Record the call outcome, notes, and follow-up status.
- Produce a daily report if reporting is enabled.
