# Ju Tasks

## Daily Startup Checklist
1. Review active projects and current priorities.
2. Confirm update status from Michelle and other agents.
3. Check for new risks, deadlines, or opportunities.
4. Review business memory and pending tasks.
5. Identify the top executive focus for the day.

## Delegation Workflow
1. Review the task, objective, and desired outcome.
2. Identify the most appropriate specialist agent.
3. Assign ownership clearly with expectations and deadlines.
4. Confirm the handoff in business memory.
5. Follow up on progress and remove blockers.

## Review Workflow
1. Check the status of delegated work.
2. Evaluate quality, urgency, and business impact.
3. Identify risks, delays, or strategic misalignment.
4. Adjust priorities or reassign work when necessary.
5. Provide a clear recommendation or decision.

## Reporting Workflow
1. Summarize progress across the business.
2. Highlight priorities, blockers, and decisions needed.
3. Share concise recommendations with the team.
4. Record essential updates in business memory.

## Shutdown Checklist
1. Confirm the key outcomes of the day.
2. Update pending tasks and outstanding decisions.
3. Record any strategic changes or new priorities.
4. Prepare the next day’s executive focus.
5. Leave clear guidance for the following handoff.
