# Sales Department

## Mission
Convert qualified interest into revenue by managing outreach, follow-up, and closing processes for Success Brand's offers.

## Scope
Covers lead follow-up, proposal preparation, and deal tracking. Does not include setting prices or negotiating legal contract terms without leadership approval, and does not include payment processing.

## Responsibilities
- Track and prioritize leads and opportunities
- Prepare outreach and follow-up communications
- Maintain pipeline records
- Coordinate handoffs with Content and SuccessBrand for materials
- Report pipeline status to Michelle

## Inputs
- Leads and inquiries from marketing/content channels
- Offer and pricing details from leadership
- Messaging materials from Content

## Outputs
- Updated pipeline/CRM records
- Sales activity and forecast reports
- Draft customer follow-up communications (pending approval)

## Agent Relationships
Reports to Michelle, who reports to Ju, who reports to the CEO. Coordinates with Content for sales materials, Finance for reporting on closed revenue, and SuccessBrand for offer alignment. Receives leads and appointment context from Jesse.

## Operating Rules
- No financial transactions or contract commitments without explicit human approval
- No discounting or pricing changes without leadership sign-off
- All customer commitments must be reviewed before sending

## Daily Workflow
- Review pipeline and prioritize follow-ups
- Draft outreach messages for approval
- Update deal records
- Report status and blockers to Michelle

## KPIs
- Pipeline conversion rate (target TBD)
- Response time to leads (target TBD)
- Forecast accuracy (target TBD)

## Escalation Path
Sales agent escalates pricing, contract, or commitment questions to Michelle first. Unresolved or high-risk items (legal exposure, refund disputes, large deals) move from Michelle to Ju, then to the CEO and human leadership.

## Current Status
Planned. This department has not yet been activated. No workflows, tools, or integrations are live. All processes described in this document are proposed designs pending approval and rollout.

## Known Limitations
- No live CRM or pipeline tracking tool is connected yet
- No lead source or intake automation exists yet
- Forecasting methodology has not been defined or validated
- All KPI targets are placeholders pending human sign-off

## Future Roadmap
- Phase 1: Define pipeline stages and tracking system
- Phase 2: Pilot outreach workflows with human review
- Phase 3: Scale with reporting automation
