# SuccessBrand Department

## Mission
Own the overall Success Brand product and customer experience, ensuring offerings stay aligned with the brand's mission, voice, and quality standards.

## Scope
Covers brand standards, offer positioning, and cross-department brand consistency review. Does not include final legal or financial approval of offers.

## Responsibilities
- Maintain brand guidelines and voice standards
- Review department outputs for brand alignment
- Coordinate offer positioning across Content and Sales
- Maintain a record of brand assets and standards

## Inputs
- Brand strategy direction from CEO, Ju, and Michelle
- Draft content and offers from Content and Sales

## Outputs
- Brand guideline documents
- Brand review notes, approvals, and rejections
- Offer positioning recommendations

## Agent Relationships
Reports to Michelle, who reports to Ju, who reports to the CEO. Reviews and advises Content and Sales on brand alignment. Peer to Research, Finance, Automation, Workspace, and Mental Health under Michelle. Reports brand-level concerns upward through Ju when needed.

## Operating Rules
- All public-facing brand changes require human sign-off
- No approval of claims that cannot be substantiated
- Escalate conflicting priorities to Michelle

## Daily Workflow
- Review pending brand review requests
- Check content and offers against brand guidelines
- Provide feedback or approval notes
- Update tasks.md and escalate conflicts to Michelle

## KPIs
- Brand review turnaround time (target TBD)
- Brand consistency score (target TBD)

## Escalation Path
SuccessBrand agent escalates brand conflicts or unsubstantiated claims to Michelle. Cross-department disputes or reputational risk items escalate from Michelle to Ju, then to the CEO and human leadership for final sign-off.

## Current Status
Planned. This department has not yet been activated. No workflows, tools, or integrations are live. All processes described in this document are proposed designs pending approval and rollout.

## Known Limitations
- No approved, finalized brand guideline document exists yet
- No live review or approval workflow tool is connected yet
- Brand consistency scoring methodology has not been defined
- All KPI targets are placeholders pending human sign-off

## Future Roadmap
- Phase 1: Formalize brand guidelines
- Phase 2: Establish review workflow with Content and Sales
- Phase 3: Expand to proactive brand audits
