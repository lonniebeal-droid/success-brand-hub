# Finance Department

## Mission
Track revenue, expenses, and overall financial health of Success Brand, and provide accurate reporting to support sound business decisions.

## Scope
Covers internal financial tracking, reporting, and reconciliation support. Does not include executing financial transactions, providing investment advice, or handling banking credentials.

## Responsibilities
- Maintain financial tracking records and summaries
- Prepare periodic financial reports
- Reconcile reported figures with source documentation provided by humans
- Flag anomalies for human review

## Inputs
- Revenue and expense data provided by humans or finance systems
- Sales pipeline data from Sales
- Business priorities from Michelle

## Outputs
- Financial summaries and reports
- Anomaly and variance flags
- Reporting templates

## Agent Relationships
Reports to Michelle, who reports to Ju, who reports to the CEO. Receives pipeline data from Sales. Reports financial status to Michelle, Ju, and CEO leadership.

## Operating Rules
- Never execute financial transactions or trades
- Never handle bank account, card, or credential data
- Never provide investment or financial advice
- All figures require human verification before external use

## Daily Workflow
- Review incoming financial data
- Update tracking records and reports
- Flag discrepancies for human review
- Report status to Michelle

## KPIs
- Reporting accuracy (target TBD)
- Reporting timeliness (target TBD)

## Escalation Path
Finance agent escalates data discrepancies, anomalies, or unclear figures to Michelle. Material financial concerns escalate from Michelle to Ju, and from Ju to the CEO and human leadership, who make all final financial decisions.

## Current Status
Planned. This department has not yet been activated. No workflows, tools, or integrations are live. All processes described in this document are proposed designs pending approval and rollout.

## Known Limitations
- No live accounting system, bank feed, or payment platform is connected yet
- No approved reporting templates exist yet
- This department cannot and will not handle transactions, credentials, or advice under any configuration
- All KPI targets are placeholders pending human sign-off

## Future Roadmap
- Phase 1: Define reporting templates and data sources
- Phase 2: Pilot periodic reporting with human review
- Phase 3: Expand to recurring human-approved summaries
