# SuccessBrand Operations SOP

Status: Planned. This SOP describes the intended process for the SuccessBrand department once activated. No step below is currently automated or live.

## Purpose
Define a repeatable process for reviewing department outputs and offers for brand consistency before they are finalized.

## Trigger
Content, Sales, or another department submits a piece for brand review, or a scheduled brand audit occurs.

## Required Inputs
- Draft content, offer, or messaging to review
- Current brand guidelines (see docs/business/successbrand-knowledge-template.md)
- Any prior review notes on related material

## Step-by-Step Process
1. Log the review request in tasks.md
2. Check the submission against brand voice, claims, and visual/style guidelines
3. Verify any factual or promotional claims are substantiated (coordinating with Research if needed)
4. Record approval, requested changes, or rejection with rationale
5. Return the decision to the submitting department
6. Track recurring issues for future guideline updates

## Quality Checks
- No unsubstantiated claims are approved
- Brand voice is consistent with approved guidelines
- Review turnaround does not block the requester without communication

## Escalation Rules
- Disputed review outcomes: escalate to Michelle
- Claims with potential legal or regulatory exposure: escalate to Michelle and Ju immediately

## Handoff Rules
- Approved items are handed back to the submitting department for publishing or sending
- Rejected items include specific, actionable revision notes

## Output Format
Review decision record with status (approved/revise/rejected), rationale, and reviewer notes.

## Metrics
- Review turnaround time (target TBD)
- Approval rate on first submission (target TBD)

## Failure Handling
If brand guidelines do not clearly cover a submission, the SuccessBrand agent escalates to Michelle for a decision rather than approving or rejecting based on assumption.
