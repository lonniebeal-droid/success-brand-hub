# Content Operating System

Status: Planned. This document describes the intended end-to-end content production system once activated. No stage below is currently automated or live.

## Purpose
Define the full pipeline for turning an idea into a published, brand-safe piece of content, including short-form video production steps.

## Idea Intake
- Ideas are logged from Michelle, Sales, SuccessBrand, or the content calendar
- Each idea is recorded with a working title, goal, and target audience

## Topic Research
- Research requests are sent to the Research department for background, statistics, or competitive context
- Findings are cited and attached to the idea record

## Script Creation
- A draft script or outline is written based on the idea and research
- Script includes a hook, core message, and call to action placeholder

## Image Prompt Creation
- Still image prompts are drafted to match the script's key moments
- Prompts specify subject, composition, and brand-safe style guidance

## Google Flow Prompt Creation
- Prompts for Google Flow (or equivalent generative video planning tool) are drafted from the script, describing scene sequencing and pacing
- These prompts are placeholders pending tool access and human approval

## Veo Prompt Creation
- Prompts for Veo (or equivalent generative video tool) are drafted for specific shots or scenes described in the script
- Prompts avoid depicting real, named individuals and follow brand-safe guidance

## Review
- Draft script, prompts, and any generated assets are reviewed internally by Content for accuracy and completeness

## Approval
- SuccessBrand reviews for brand alignment per docs/business/successbrand-operations-sop.md
- Mental Health reviews if the content touches wellbeing, stress, or sensitive topics per docs/business/mental-health-content-sop.md
- A human gives final publishing approval

## Publishing Handoff
- Approved content and assets are handed off to Michelle or the designated publishing owner per docs/business/agent-handoffs.md

## Reuse and Repurposing
- Published pieces are tagged and archived for reuse across formats and channels
- Repurposed content still requires a lightweight brand check before republishing

## 9:16 Standards
- Vertical video content targets a 9:16 aspect ratio for short-form platforms
- Key text and subjects are kept within the safe zone to avoid platform UI overlap
- Captions are included by default for accessibility

## SuccessBrand Brand Checks
- Every piece is checked against docs/business/successbrand-knowledge-template.md brand voice, approved claims, and disallowed claims sections before publishing

## Mental Health Safety Review
- Any content referencing stress, wellbeing, health, or crisis topics requires Mental Health review before it can be approved, regardless of format
