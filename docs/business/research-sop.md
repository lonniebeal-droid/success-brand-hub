# Research Department SOP

Status: Planned. This SOP describes the intended process for the Research department once activated. No step below is currently automated or live.

## Purpose
Define a repeatable process for producing accurate, source-cited research briefs.

## Trigger
A research request is submitted by Michelle, Content, Sales, Finance, or SuccessBrand.

## Required Inputs
- Research question or topic
- Intended use of the findings
- Any deadline or priority level

## Step-by-Step Process
1. Log the request in tasks.md with requester and deadline
2. Clarify scope and intended use if ambiguous
3. Gather information from publicly available, reputable sources
4. Validate and cross-check key claims across sources
5. Summarize findings with citations in a research brief
6. Deliver the brief to the requesting department
7. Archive the brief and sources in the knowledge base

## Quality Checks
- Every material claim has a cited source
- No fabricated or unverifiable data
- Summary is substantially shorter than and distinct from source material

## Escalation Rules
- Requests involving legal, medical, or financial claims: escalate to Michelle before delivering findings
- Conflicting or unverifiable sources: escalate to Michelle for guidance

## Handoff Rules
- Briefs delivered to Content include suggested use context and citations
- Briefs delivered to Sales include competitive or market framing relevant to outreach

## Output Format
Research brief with sections for question, summary, key findings, sources, and confidence level.

## Metrics
- Requests completed per period (target TBD)
- Source citation completeness (target TBD)

## Failure Handling
If reliable sources cannot be found or a claim cannot be verified, the Research agent reports this limitation explicitly in the brief rather than presenting unverified information as fact.
