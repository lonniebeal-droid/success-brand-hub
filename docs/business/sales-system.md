# Sales Operating System

Status: Planned. This document describes the intended end-to-end sales system once activated. No stage below is currently automated or live.

## Purpose
Define the full pipeline for moving a contact from initial lead to a closed outcome.

## Lead Intake
- Leads arrive from Jesse, marketing/content channels, or direct inquiry
- Each lead is logged with source, contact method, and initial context

## Qualification
- Leads are checked against defined qualification criteria (to be set by leadership), such as fit, intent, and readiness
- Unqualified leads are logged and routed to nurture or closed-lost

## Scoring
- Qualified leads receive a placeholder score based on fit and engagement signals (scoring model to be defined by leadership)
- Scores are used to prioritize outreach order, not to guarantee outcomes

## Outreach
- Initial outreach message is drafted using approved Content materials
- All outreach is reviewed by a human before sending until the department is fully activated

## Follow-Up Cadence
- A defined sequence of follow-up touchpoints is planned (cadence and channel mix to be set by leadership)
- Cadence pauses if the lead requests no further contact

## Proposal
- For qualified, engaged leads, a proposal is drafted using approved pricing and offer terms
- Proposals require human approval before sending

## Appointment Handoff
- When a lead is ready for a live conversation, appointment details are handed off per docs/business/agent-handoffs.md

## Pipeline Stages
- New, Qualifying, Nurture, Proposal Sent, Negotiation, Closed-Won, Closed-Lost (stage definitions to be finalized by leadership)

## Lost-Lead Review
- Closed-lost leads are reviewed periodically for patterns and handed to Research for lost-lead analysis

## Reporting
- Pipeline status, conversion rates, and forecast summaries are reported to Michelle per docs/business/kpi-catalog.md and docs/business/weekly-executive-report.md
