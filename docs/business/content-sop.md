# Content Department SOP

Status: Planned. This SOP describes the intended process for the Content department once activated. No step below is currently automated or live.

## Purpose
Define a repeatable process for producing on-brand content from request to publication-ready draft.

## Trigger
A content request is logged by Michelle, Sales, SuccessBrand, or the content calendar cycle.

## Required Inputs
- Content request or calendar slot
- Relevant brand guidelines from SuccessBrand
- Supporting research from Research (if applicable)
- Target channel and format

## Step-by-Step Process
1. Log the request in tasks.md with priority and due date
2. Confirm scope, format, and any required research with the requester
3. Draft the content piece
4. Self-review against brand guidelines and factual accuracy
5. Submit to SuccessBrand for brand alignment review
6. Incorporate feedback and finalize the draft
7. Hand off the approved draft per docs/business/agent-handoffs.md
8. Archive the final version and update memory.md

## Quality Checks
- Brand voice and tone match approved guidelines
- No unverified claims or unlicensed material
- Formatting matches the target channel's standards

## Escalation Rules
- Ambiguous scope or conflicting priorities: escalate to Michelle
- Suspected legal or compliance issue: escalate to Michelle and Ju immediately

## Handoff Rules
- Approved content is handed off to the requesting department (Sales or SuccessBrand) or to Michelle for publishing coordination
- Handoffs must include the final draft, brand review notes, and any usage restrictions

## Output Format
Markdown or plain text draft with a metadata header (title, channel, requester, status, review notes).

## Metrics
- Requests completed on time (target TBD)
- Revision cycles per piece (target TBD)

## Failure Handling
If a deadline will be missed or a request cannot be completed within scope, the Content agent logs the blocker in tasks.md and notifies Michelle before the due date, rather than delivering incomplete or unreviewed work.
