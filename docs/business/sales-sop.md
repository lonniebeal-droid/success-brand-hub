# Sales Department SOP

Status: Planned. This SOP describes the intended process for the Sales department once activated. No step below is currently automated or live.

## Purpose
Define a repeatable process for moving a lead from intake to a closed outcome (won, lost, or nurtured).

## Trigger
A new lead or inquiry is received, or a scheduled follow-up comes due.

## Required Inputs
- Lead contact and inquiry details
- Offer and pricing information from leadership
- Relevant content/materials from Content

## Step-by-Step Process
1. Log the lead in the pipeline record with source and initial notes
2. Qualify the lead against defined criteria (see docs/business/sales-system.md)
3. Draft an outreach or follow-up message for human review
4. Send approved communication and log the interaction
5. Update pipeline stage based on response
6. Prepare a proposal if the lead qualifies, for human approval before sending
7. Hand off closed-won leads to Finance for revenue tracking, and closed-lost leads to Research for lost-lead review
8. Update memory.md with notable patterns

## Quality Checks
- All commitments and pricing reviewed by a human before being sent
- Pipeline record is accurate and current
- No communication sent without required approval

## Escalation Rules
- Pricing exceptions or contract terms: escalate to Michelle
- Refund disputes or legal concerns: escalate to Michelle and Ju immediately

## Handoff Rules
- Closed-won deals are hand off to Finance with deal value and terms
- Marketing-qualified content needs are handed off to Content

## Output Format
Structured pipeline record entries (stage, value placeholder, next action, owner) and draft communications pending approval.

## Metrics
- Lead response time (target TBD)
- Conversion rate by stage (target TBD)

## Failure Handling
If a lead cannot be qualified or a required approval is not obtained in time, the Sales agent logs the blocker in tasks.md, marks the lead as pending, and notifies Michelle rather than proceeding without approval.
